Traditional Music

Malay

Traditional Malay music uses music instruments such as the rebab, gong, and gamelan. The rebab is a stringed instrument, while the gong is a percussion instrument that adds depth and texture to the music. Gamelan is a set of percussion instruments that create a unique, melodic sound.

Chinese

Traditional Chinese music is a diverse and ancient art form characterized by a rich tapestry of instruments, including the erhu, guzheng, pipa, and dizi. It features pentatonic scales, expressive ornamentation, and rhythmic depth provided by percussion instruments like drums and gongs.

Indian

Traditional Indian music: based on ragas (melodic frameworks) and talas (rhythmic cycles); features instruments like sitar, tabla, and flute; emphasizes improvisation and spiritual significance.
