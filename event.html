Date: 25 May 2024

Time: 12pm

Kabaddi Tournament

Date: 19-20 November 2024

Time: 9am

Venue: Sahodaya School Complex

Details: Kabaddi is a high-energy team sport originating from ancient India. Players take turns raiding opponents' territory while chanting "kabaddi" to tag them. The game requires physical strength, agility, and strategic teamwork, making it both thrilling to watch and play.

Hari Raya Open House

Date: 19 May 2024

Time: 3pm-6pm

Venue: UNGCMYB HQ, Petaling Jaya

Details: Hari Raya Open House is a cherished tradition in Malaysia and other Muslim-majority countries, held during the Eid al-Fitr celebration marking the end of Ramadan. It's a time for friends, family, and neighbors of all backgrounds to come together, enjoy delicious traditional foods, exchange greetings, and strengthen bonds of community and friendship.

Malay Traditional Game for Hari Raya

Date: 13 May 2024

Time: 12pm

Venue: Old Basketball Court

Details: Gasing, also known as spinning tops, is a traditional game popular in Malaysia and other Southeast Asian countries. Players spin wooden tops with strings, competing to keep them spinning the longest or to knock opponents' tops out of a designated area. It's not only a source of fun and entertainment but also carries cultural significance, often played during festivals and social gatherings.

Persembahan Wayang Kulit

Date: 9 August 2024

Time: 11am - 3:30pm

Venue: Dewan D MAEPS, Serdang

Details: Wayang Kulit, a cherished cultural tradition in Southeast Asia, showcases intricate leather puppets telling captivating tales from folklore and mythology. Accompanied by music and narration, these performances preserve rich heritage while captivating audiences with their enchanting storytelling.
